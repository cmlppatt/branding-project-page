Flashing lights, groovy music, and great times. Party Anthem Concert is a made up event targeting young adults fans of indie/alternative late-night music. Part of the french marketing campaign, both the poster and concert schedule use a dreamy colour palette paired with bold saturated graphics to hype up the event.
